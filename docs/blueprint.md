# **App Name**: Workforce Pro

## Core Features:

- Candidate CV Analysis: Upload and parse candidate CVs to automatically extract key data such as name, age, qualifications, skills, and work experience. The parsing of the CVs is performed by an LLM tool, that also suggests an area of specialization according to the CV
- Candidate Management: Maintain a database of potential candidates, making it easy to search, filter, and manage them for various job opportunities.
- Workforce Management: Manage all contracted workers details, including information regarding each worker's compensation. Generate and maintain digital contracts. Handle a variety of HR tasks, such as disciplinary procedures, pay slips, and work permits.
- Service and Contract Management: Track both fixed (manpower) and event-based services for clients, including creation, modification, status updates (active, completed, suspended).
- Attendance Tracking: Log daily attendance for workers, upload signed attendance sheets, and generate attendance reports.
- Billing and Invoicing: Create and manage client billing, including the creation of daily, weekly or monthly invoices, configurable price per day and incorporating shift differentials (10%, 20%). Allows the admin to manage parameters such as the cost per day. Calculate employee-related taxes and contributions (INSS, IRT, etc.).
- Reporting and Analytics: Generate a range of reports with graphical representations, including worker distribution, financial summaries, and service utilization, useful for strategic planning and operational insights.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep teal (#008080) to convey professionalism and reliability.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated teal (#F0F8FF) provides a clean, corporate backdrop.
- Accent color: A warm yellow-orange (#FFC145), used sparingly, can draw attention to calls to action.
- Headline font: 'Poppins', a sans-serif, known for its geometric shapes and modern style; body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif providing readability and warmth.
- Use professional and consistent icons across the platform to represent different functionalities and departments, aiding in quick navigation and understanding.
- Maintain a clean and structured layout with clear divisions for different sections such as employee management, invoicing, and reporting. Emphasize key data points with appropriate visual hierarchy.
- Incorporate subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience, such as loading indicators, confirmation messages, and smooth navigation between sections.
